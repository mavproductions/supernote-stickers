    📋 How to use your sticker pack

    1. Download the .snstk file above.
    2. Connect your Supernote to your computer (USB or Supernote cloud).
    3. Copy the file to your Supernote's EXPORT folder.
    4. On your Supernote, open Settings › Stickers and import.
