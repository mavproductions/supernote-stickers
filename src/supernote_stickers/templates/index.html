    How to use your sticker pack

    1. Download the .snstk file above.
    2. Connect your Supernote to your computer via USB or Supernote Cloud.
    3. Copy the file to your Supernote's EXPORT folder.
    4. On your Supernote, open a Note, tap the Stickers button in the toolbar, and import.
